About Okere District

Our History

The Okere District Assembly was established to serve the people of Okere with dedication and transparency. Over the years, we have grown to become a leading example of effective local governance in the region.

Our journey has been marked by continuous improvement in service delivery, infrastructure development, and community engagement. We remain committed to building a better future for all residents of Okere District.

Our Mission

To provide efficient, effective, and accountable governance that improves the quality of life for all residents through sustainable development and community participation.

Our Vision

To be a model district assembly recognized for excellence in public service, innovation, and community development that creates opportunities for all.

Our Values

Transparency: We believe in open and honest communication with our stakeholders.

Accountability: We take responsibility for our actions and decisions.

Excellence: We strive for the highest standards in all our services.

Community Focus: We put the needs of our community at the center of everything we do.

Leadership

The Okere District Assembly is led by a dedicated team of professionals committed to serving the public interest. Our leadership brings together expertise in various fields including public administration, finance, development planning, and community engagement.
